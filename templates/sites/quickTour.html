Quick Add ToDos

Use the Quick Add to assign ToDo's to people and due dates. It can handle many different ways to assign due dates. For example: "Phase III due in 5 days by Bill and Sally"

ToDo List

Drag and Drop the ToDo's into the right order. Click the check mark to mark an ToDo complete.

ToDo Details

Click the "View ToDo Details" button to see info about a ToDo and to edit the ToDo.

Calendar

Drag ToDo's onto the calendar to assign or reassign a due date. Also, drag ToDo's already on the calendar around to reassign the due date.

Discussions

Visit the "Discuss" tab to start discussions with your group members. You can drag ToDo's onto discussions so all your group members know exactly which ToDo's you're talking about.
